HRC and Girdlers’ UK Fellowship

The 2024 HRC and Girdlers’ UK Fellowship, providing a post-doctoral fellowship opportunity at the University of Oxford, is now open for applications. The award offers a 2-year Post-Doctoral Fellowship at the University of Oxford (starting in 2024), and 1 year of further support on return to NZ.

Stavros Petrou, Professor of Health Economics at the Nuffield Department of Primary Care Health Services, has agreed to be contacted as a potential supervisor for applicants in the area of health economics. See the documents at the link below for details and contact information.

For details and application forms, see the HRC website: https://gateway.hrc.govt.nz/funding/career-development-awards/2024-girdlers-uk-fellowship. Closing date for applications is 4 July 2023.
